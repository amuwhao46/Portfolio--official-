Hello! My name is Oke-Oghene Amuwha, but I normally go by Oke Amuwha. I was born in Nigeria and moved to the United States in 2004. After I graduate I hope to work in computer software engineering, helping in machine learning or front end development.

I've always had an interest in how and why stuff just seems to work. When I was younger I would always look up at the stars in amazement at whats out there. Constantly learning about deep space and the mysteries thats out there. Nowawdays that sense of wonder has come rocketing home. I'm more focused on the mind and it's interpretation of reality and the parallels it has with the technology we interface with. How is it that a certain vibration of particles makes the mind interpret it as music? Likewise, how does tapping a certain part of my phone display cause the flashlight to light up? I believe we will have, at the very least, the beginnings of an understanding of the hard problem of consciousness. And I want to be a part of that discovery, either contributing my own observations and measurements or indirectly through my field of study. Either way it's amazing how interconnected a lot of things seem to be, even with this delicate balance it all just works.
